What to Expect from Ski Lessons

When you enroll your child in ski lessons with me, here's what you can expect:

  • Personalized Instruction: I tailor each lesson to suit the individual needs of my students. Whether your child is a complete novice or has some experience, I will adjust the pace and content of the lessons accordingly.
  • Focus on Fundamentals: In the initial lessons, I emphasize essential skills such as stopping, turning, and balancing. These foundational techniques are crucial for building a solid skiing experience.
  • Building Confidence: I understand that every child learns at their own pace. My goal is to ensure your child feels confident and excited about skiing, whether they're gliding down the gentle slopes or tackling a slightly steeper hill.

Tips for Parents

To help your child have the best possible skiing experience, here are a few tips for parents:

  • Dress Appropriately: Ensure your child is dressed in warm, waterproof clothing. Layers are key! Make sure they have proper ski gloves, helmets, and goggles for safety and comfort.
  • Stay Positive: Encourage your child to have a positive attitude toward skiing. Celebrate their progress, no matter how small, and remind them that it's okay to make mistakes along the way.
  • Provide Hydration and Snacks: Skiing can be exhausting! Keep your child hydrated and offer healthy snacks during breaks to keep their energy up.
