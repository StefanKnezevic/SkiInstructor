CLASSES

Classes for children

The recommended age for starting with lessons is 3+ years old. Kids primarily learn by imitating the instructor. Children start with exercises on a flat base, and then move onto gentle slopes. Since they are younger they typically require more animation, so the training is based on games etc. in order to capture their attention and gain their interest. With older children, focus shifts to working on technique and correcting errors. In addition to ski knowledge, children also master the rules of behavior on the ski slopes and the use of cable cars.

Classes for adults

The verbal method of training is much more prevalent in training sessions for adults. Adult beginners often experience fear and require assurance and support from the instructor. The instructor must provide them with safety with assistance and a gradual transition to the next tasks. With more experienced skiers, focus is put on technique correction, with exercises being done to correct mistakes and then also learn new techniques.
